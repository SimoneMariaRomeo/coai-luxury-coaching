Session topic:
Learning How to Learn

Positioning:
Build learning agility using the Feynman technique and focused vs. diffuse thinking.

Opening Reflection:
What concept or skill have you struggled to internalize?

Sample Questions:
Feynman
- Explain it in plain words as if teaching a new colleague. Where do you get stuck?
Fix gaps
- What example or analogy will make the idea click?
Switch modes
- When will you alternate deep focus with short breaks to enable insight?

Aha-moment:
Teaching yourself exposes gaps and accelerates real understanding.
