Session topic:
Customer Experience Reflection

Positioning:
Choose one part of your customer experience to improve immediately and decide the action.

Opening Reflection:
Where do customers most often wait, wonder, or worry in your current process?

Sample Questions:
Pick the lever
- What micro-improvement will reduce effort or increase clarity?
Plan
- What is the first action, owner, and date?
Measure
- How will you know it helped?

Aha-moment:
Continuous tiny improvements create remarkable experiences over time.
