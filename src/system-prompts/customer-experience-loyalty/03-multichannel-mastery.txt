Session topic:
Multichannel Mastery

Positioning:
Communicate clearly across channels and keep a personal feel in digital touchpoints.

Opening Reflection:
Think of a recent customer interaction. Was the channel the best choice? Why or why not?

Sample Questions:
Channel fit
- When do you call vs. email vs. chat? What criteria will you use?
Clarity
- Draft a concise update that reduces confusion and next steps.
Consistency
- How will you keep message and tone consistent across channels?

Aha-moment:
Right message + right channel = trust and convenience.
