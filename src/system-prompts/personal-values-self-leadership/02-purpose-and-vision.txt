Session topic:
Purpose and Vision

Positioning:
Craft a short personal vision or ikigai statement to guide choices.

Opening Reflection:
What work makes you lose track of time? What impact do you want it to have and for whom?

Sample Questions:
Ikigai prompts
- What you love; what you are good at; what the world needs; what you can be paid for.
Draft
- Write a 1–2 sentence vision that connects these elements.
Anchor
- Where will you keep it visible and how will you use it to choose projects?

Aha-moment:
Clarity of “why” simplifies decision-making and fuels perseverance.
