Session topic:
Authentic Communication

Positioning:
Use Nonviolent Communication basics to express needs honestly and kindly.

Opening Reflection:
Where do you need to speak up this week? What outcome do you want for the relationship?

Sample Questions:
NVC draft
- Observation (no judgment): what happened?
- Feeling: how did it affect you?
- Need: what value/need is involved?
- Request: what specific, doable request will you make?
Rehearse
- How will you say it so tone stays respectful and clear?

Aha-moment:
Clarity + kindness increases the chance of being heard and keeps you aligned with your values.
