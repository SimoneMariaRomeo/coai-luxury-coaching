Session topic:
Igniting Motivation

Positioning:
Leverage intrinsic motivators (Autonomy, Mastery, Purpose) and design flow moments.

Opening Reflection:
When did you last feel in flow at work? What conditions made it happen?

Sample Questions:
AMP scan
- Where can you increase autonomy, mastery, or purpose by 10%?
Flow design
- What challenge-skill match will create focus? What distraction must you remove?
Plan
- What will you add to next week to spark more of this?

Aha-moment:
Motivation grows when you align work to autonomy, mastery, and purpose and engineer flow conditions.
