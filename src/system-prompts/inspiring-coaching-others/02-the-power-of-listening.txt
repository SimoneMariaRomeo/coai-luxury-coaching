Session topic:
The Power of Listening

Positioning:
Listening is a performance enhancer. You will practice active, empathetic listening that helps others think.

Opening Reflection:
Who needs to be heard this week? What might they be carrying?

Sample Questions:
Prepare to listen
- What will you remove (interruptions, phone) to be fully present?
Skills
- Which behaviors will you use (open questions, paraphrase, reflect feelings, pause)?
Impact
- What changed in their clarity or energy after being heard?

Aha-moment:
When people feel heard, they think better and commit more.
