Session topic:
Coaching in Action (Practice)

Positioning:
Run a short peer coaching session using mostly questions and reflections; notice learning on both sides.

Opening Reflection:
Who will be your practice partner and what topic will you bring?

Sample Questions:
Structure
- 10 min coach A, 10 min coach B, 5 min debrief.
Coach moves
- Ask, mirror, summarize, invite options; withhold advice unless asked.
Debrief
- What did you learn as coach? As coachee? What question landed most?
